# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **With Product Managers:** Clarify requirements and acceptance criteria
- **With Project Managers:** Report progress and identify blockers
- **With Designers:** Implement design specifications and discuss feasibility
- **With Technical Writers:** Review documentation for technical accuracy
- **With Security Leads:** Implement security recommendations and fixes

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **With Developers:** Define requirements and validate solutions
- **With Project Managers:** Align on roadmap priorities and timelines
- **With Designers:** Collaborate on user research and feature design
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Incorporate customer feedback into roadmap
- **With Security Leads:** Evaluate security implications of features

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **With Developers:** Track progress and remove impediments
- **With Product Managers:** Align on priorities and manage scope changes
- **With All Team Members:** Facilitate communication and maintain transparency
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Coordinate incident response and communication
- **With Technical Writers:** Ensure documentation deliverables are tracked

---

## Designers / User Experience Lead

### Role Summary
Designers and User Experience (UX) Leads ensure that products meet user-centric design standards and accessibility requirements. They participate in feature discussions and validate interface usability throughout the product lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Ensure user-centric design standards across all features
- Participate in feature prioritization discussions with Product Managers
- Validate interface usability and accessibility compliance
- Document UX decisions and design rationale
- Create and maintain design systems and component libraries
- Conduct user research and usability testing

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Maintain consistency across product interfaces
- Balance user needs with technical constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions with Developers and Product Managers
- User research findings and recommendations
- Design artifacts (wireframes, prototypes, style guides)
- Accessibility audit reports

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **With Developers:** Deliver design artifacts and collaborate on implementation feasibility
- **With Product Managers:** Clarify requirements and participate in feature discussions
- **With Stakeholders:** Engage in design review cycles and validate solutions
- **With Technical Writers:** Ensure UI terminology consistency in documentation

---

## Support / Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success teams provide critical feedback from customer interactions and help ensure successful adoption of products. They act as the voice of the customer within project teams.

### Responsibilities
- Collect and analyze feedback from customer inquiries and support tickets
- Feed common issues and patterns into retrospectives and risk registers
- Assist in drafting release notes and troubleshooting guides
- Validate that new features address customer pain points
- Track customer satisfaction metrics and escalate concerns
- Maintain customer-facing knowledge base articles

### Goals
- Improve customer satisfaction and reduce support volume
- Enable smooth feature adoption and rollout
- Provide actionable customer insights to development teams

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback reports and trend analysis
- Support ticket escalations and incident reports
- Release readiness reviews
- Knowledge base updates

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **With Project Managers:** Coordinate incident communication and status updates
- **With Technical Writers:** Update knowledge base and documentation
- **With Developers:** Provide reproduction steps and context for reported issues
- **With Product Managers:** Share customer insights to inform roadmap priorities

---

## Technical Writer / Documentation Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Writers and Documentation Leads maintain comprehensive project and process documentation, ensuring information is accurate, accessible, and consistent across all artifacts.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain project documentation and process guides
- Prepare templates for consistent artifact creation across teams
- Review documentation as part of release and retrospective cycles
- Ensure documentation meets quality and accessibility standards
- Coordinate documentation reviews with subject matter experts
- Archive and version documentation appropriately

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate documentation for all stakeholders
- Reduce time to onboard new team members
- Ensure documentation stays current with product changes

### Typical Communication
- Documentation review requests and feedback
- Style guide updates and template releases
- Release notes and user guides
- Documentation health reports

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **With Developers:** Gather technical details for accuracy and completeness
- **With Product Managers:** Understand feature scope and user stories
- **With Project Managers:** Align on documentation deliverables and timelines
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Coordinate on knowledge base content
- **With Designers:** Ensure UI terminology consistency

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure that security considerations are integrated throughout the project lifecycle, from initial planning through release and ongoing operations.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct threat modeling and security risk analysis
- Ensure compliance with security policies and regulations
- Review release checklists for potential vulnerabilities
- Maintain incident response readiness and runbooks
- Provide security training and awareness to team members
- Coordinate security audits and penetration testing

### Goals
- Prevent security vulnerabilities from reaching production
- Ensure rapid response to security incidents
- Maintain regulatory compliance and security best practices

### Typical Communication
- Security review findings and recommendations
- Threat model documentation
- Incident response coordination
- Security policy updates

### Cross-functional Collaboration
- **With Developers:** Advise on secure coding practices and review implementations
- **With Project Managers:** Participate in planning and release reviews, escalate issues per runbook
- **With Product Managers:** Assess security implications of feature requirements
- **With Support/Customer Success:** Coordinate response to security-related customer reports
